## Full description (English)

```
Alif Taxi is a modern taxi-hailing service for the Talas Region of
Kyrgyzstan. We operate in three villages: Talas, Kirovka, and Pokrovka.
Book a ride in under a minute, see the price before you order, and
track your driver on the map in real time.

KEY FEATURES

• In-village rides — fixed prices, no surprises
• Inter-village trips — choose your destination and see the fare instantly
• Round-trip option — driver takes you, waits, and brings you back
• Live map showing your driver's location and ETA
• Notification when the driver arrives — even with the app minimised
• One-tap call to the driver
• Driver rating after each ride — help us maintain quality
• Full ride history with details
• Simple phone-number login via SMS code (no passwords)

OUR DRIVERS
Local drivers who know every street in Talas, Kirovka, and Pokrovka.
Every driver has a verified profile with photo, vehicle model, and
rating from passengers.

TRANSPARENT PRICING
You see the price before you order. No hidden fees, no surge
pricing. The round-trip option adds a fixed percentage that's
shown upfront.

SUPPORT
Reach us anytime via WhatsApp from the Profile screen.

PRIVACY
We collect only the data we need to dispatch a taxi: your phone
number for login, your name for the driver, and your GPS location
at the moment of ordering. Full details are in our privacy policy
(linked in your Profile).

ABOUT US
Alif Taxi is an independent regional service built by and for the
local community. We work directly with regional drivers, not through
global aggregators — which lets us keep prices low and availability
high in small villages.

Support contacts:
• WhatsApp: +996 509 397 226
• Email: ernazarovmaksat4@gmail.com
```
